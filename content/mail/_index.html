AOSC Hermes

AOSC Hermes is a community-maintaiend e-mail service for project and community contributors. Hermes users are given an @aosc.io suffixed e-mail address.

Contributors, you may login to your inbox or manage your account via the links below. You may also use your preferred mail client, autodiscovery functionality is enabled.
