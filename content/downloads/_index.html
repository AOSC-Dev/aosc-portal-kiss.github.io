Here you will find downloads for AOSC OS versions, and other projects produced by the community.

AOSC OS

Our mainline distribution, a general-purpose distribution built to simplify installation and configuration, with a rich repository for both free and open source, as well as proprietary software. These releases target desktop and mobile devices, servers, and development boards.

AOSC OS/Retro

Our Retro distribution, sharing package configurations and design principles from our mainline distribution. These releases target older and "ancient" hardware with limited performance and storage. These releases are not frequently updated but receives security and bug-fix updates.
